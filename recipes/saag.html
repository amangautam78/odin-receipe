Saag

Saag is a traditional Indian recipe from the state of Punjab. This recipe is mentioned in holy Indian scriptures (Ayurveda) and is more than 2500 years old. During the winter, greens such as spinach, fenugreek leaves, and mustard greens grow in abundance.

Ingredients

  • Palak
  • Meethi
  • Paneer 500gm - 4 people
  • pyaza - 250gm
  • Tmoato - 500gm
  • haldi
  • Lal mirch
  • garam masala
  • Paneer masala
  • Tej patta

Steps to make the dish

  1. Boiled palak and meethi
  2. Fry pyaz & tomata
  3. Put all the masala
  4. Stir it till completely fried
  5. add water 500ml
  6. Add paneer cubes
